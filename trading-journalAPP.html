How to connect your cTrader account
Follow these steps to get your Client ID, Client Secret and Account ID.
1
Create a Spotware developer account
Go to connect.spotware.com and sign up for a free account. This is the official developer portal for cTrader — it's separate from your trading account.
2
Create a new application
Once logged in, click Applications in the top menu, then click New Application. Fill in the form:
  • Name — anything you like, e.g. Trade 333 Journal
  • Redirect URI — enter exactly: https://localhost
  • Leave all other fields as their defaults
Click Create to save.
3
Copy your Client ID and Client Secret
After creating the app you'll see a details page. Copy both values and paste them into the fields below:
  • Client ID — a long alphanumeric string
  • Client Secret — another long string (treat this like a password)
4
Find your cTrader Account ID
Open the cTrader desktop or web platform. Then:
  • Click your account name or profile icon in the top corner
  • Go to Settings → Account Details
  • Your Account ID is the numeric ID shown (e.g. 12345678)
  • Note whether it is a Demo or Live account
5
Connect below
Fill in all four fields below, then click Connect cTrader. A link will appear — open it in your browser, log in to cTrader, and authorise the app. You'll be redirected to a blank page with localhost/?code=… in the address bar. Copy that full URL and paste it into the confirmation field.

Help & FAQs
Quick answers for using your trading journal and keeping your data organised.
Useful Tips
  • Use My Trades to review, filter, export, and manage your trade history.
  • The Refresh button imports new CSV files from your selected folder and skips duplicates.
  • Use the table sidebar tabs for Columns, Filters, and Actions.
  • AI Insights can answer questions using your journal data once connected in Settings.
FAQs
  • Where is my data saved? Data is stored locally in this browser unless you export it.
  • Can I undo mistakes? Use the undo button on My Trades after accidental uploads or deletions.
  • Why is folder import unavailable? Some browsers limit folder access. Use Chrome/Edge-style folder access or manual CSV upload.
  • How do I change the theme? Go to Settings, choose a theme from the dropdown, then click Apply.
